Teaching Philosophy

"True engineering mastery relies on connecting abstract theoretical stability limitations directly to physical embedded constraints."

My pedagogical methodology combines rigorous programmatic verification with hands-on exploratory hardware execution. I seek to construct open, low-overhead environments where students can systematically deconstruct machine learning abstractions into concrete deterministic real-world calculations, thereby empowering future academic discovery tracks.
