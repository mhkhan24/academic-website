Research Areas

My collective core research paradigm operates at the nexus of high-speed computation and reliable deployment constraints. Below are the specific tracking domains I investigate within our lab.

Safe Reinforcement Learning

Developing structural control barrier functions derived from dynamic equations to guarantee sample convergence within deep policy systems while structurally minimizing runtime hazard violations.

Learn More

Hardware-Aware Optimization

Investigating multi-bit dynamic quantization configurations across neural pipelines allowing direct compilation onto mixed-signal localized processing chips without sacrificing inference performance metrics.

Learn More

Decentralized Multi-Agent Swarms

Analyzing asynchronous algebraic consensus algorithms applied to heterogeneous aerial and terrestrial physical agents running localized state tracking under communication dropouts.

Learn More

Robust Vision-Language-Action

Constructing multi-modal topological representation models mapping high-level user intent vectors into sparse, executable actuation arrays across multi-joint robotic structures.

Learn More

Adversarial Defenses at Edge

Evaluating systematic bounds on white-box input perturbation schemes targeting physical perceptual layers, developing geometric smoothing defenses to insulate structural classifiers.

Learn More

Adaptive Non-Stationary Control

Applying runtime sliding-mode filtering heuristics onto active neural system feedback components to secure trajectory accuracy within volatile fluid mechanical mediums.
