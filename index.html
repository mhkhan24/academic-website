Biography

Welcome! I am a 4th-year PhD Candidate in the Department of Physics at the University of Houston.

Research Interests

  • Robust Reinforcement Learning: Safe exploration, state abstraction, and policy transfer under variable dynamics.
  • Edge Computing & TinyML: Quantization and optimization of deep architectures for deployment on embedded microcontroller platforms.
  • Cyber-Physical Systems: Scalable, decentralized coordination frameworks for heterogeneous multi-agent operations.
  • Stochastic Control: Real-time trajectory generation and optimization under severe localized sensing noise.
  • Human-AI Interaction: Interpretability of deep neural networks during collaborative human-in-the-loop control paradigms.
